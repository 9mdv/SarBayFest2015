#SARBAYFEST2015


How to get there !

Sarbay Fest is held at Gumasa Beach in Glan, Sarangani Province.

You may reach Sarangani Province by plane via Manila, Cebu, or Iloilo to General Santos City Airport. You may also access Sarangani via land travel from Davao City.

to get to Gumasa from General Santos City you proceed to the Glan terminal across KCC Mall of Gensan. Take a Van ride from Gensan to Poblacion, Glan and it will approximate a travel time of 1 hour if travel is non-stop.
