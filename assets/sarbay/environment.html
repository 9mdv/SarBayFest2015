#SARBAYFEST2015


ENVIRONMENT

Through the years the advocacy of Sarbay is to protect Sarangani Bay and keep it clean. Efforts such as mangrove planting, scubasurero, crown of thorns pick-up, and even campaigns and awareness of the wildlife found on the bay have are conducted during the Sarbay.

This year, the province together with the municipality of Glan is supporting the ordinance on charging environmental fees as you enter Gumasa. For this years' Sarbay, we will be charging everyone an environmental fee of P30.00. This will include a trash bag and we expect you to be responsible with your garbage.

At the end of your stay in Sarbay, please submit your trash bag in our collection station. If you hit 5Kilos you can get a token for cleaning the beach. We are also inviting everyone for a Clean Up Party on May 17 at 6AM. Become one with us, and be a Responsible Sarbay Citizen!
