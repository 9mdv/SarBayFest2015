HISTORY

Sarangani is known for its pristine beaches and ecologically abundant bay. Sarangani Bay Festival or “SARBAY FEST” started in 2006. From 3,000 guests on its first year, the festival has become the Biggest Beach Festival in the entire Mindanao with over 70,000 visitors in 2013, and 120,000 in 2014. It has become the Summer Sports Haven of sports enthusiasts from all over the country. It kicks off the challenging 15 kilometer “Swim Across the Bay” team competition. Other sports events include Beach Volleyball, Beach Football, Jet ski Competition, Sarangani Triathlon, Beach Zumba and many others.

Sarbay Fest also advocates the preservation of the rich biodiverse ecosystem of the bay with activities such as mangrove planting, crown of thorns removal, and scubasurero.

Today, “SARBAY FEST” is recognized by the Association of Tourism Officers of the Philippines (ATOP) and Department of Tourism (DOT) as the Pearl Awardee for Best Tourism Event for Sports, Adventure, and Wellness. This event is attracting beach party goers from all over the country with nightly bands and DJ’s. It is the most looked forward event of the summer season in Mindanao.
